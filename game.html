The games i played : (more)

Valorant

Valorant is a competitive game that I've really gotten into—I'm currently ranked Platinum and have put in around 2000 hours of playtime. My main character is Clove, and I’ve really honed my skills with them. The game itself is a tactical FPS where two teams of five go head-to-head, with one team attacking to plant a bomb (the spike) and the other defending. What really sets Valorant apart is the unique abilities each agent has, which can completely change the flow of a match if used correctly. I've spent a lot of time learning the ins and outs of the game, especially with Clove, and it's been a great experience climbing the ranks and improving my gameplay. Whether it's hitting those clutch shots or perfectly timing an ability, there's always something to work on, which keeps the game exciting.

Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is the one game I've been hooked on since its launch, and I'm still playing it. I absolutely love the characters and the depth of the story. The rich lore of the game is what draws me in the most—it’s truly captivating and keeps me engaged every time I play.

Once Human

I’m really passionate about Once Human. It’s not just the gameplay that captivates me; the story is absolutely incredible. The depth of the narrative, the way it’s intricately woven into the gameplay, and the rich lore make every moment in the game immersive and engaging. The graphics are also stunning, with detailed environments and atmospheric effects that enhance the overall experience. The world-building and character development are top-notch, adding layers of intrigue and excitement to the visually impressive game.

Rdr2

I’m deeply captivated by the story in Red Dead Redemption 2. The rich narrative and complex characters are incredibly engaging, and it's the unfolding story that truly keeps me invested and coming back to the game. Each twist and turn in the plot adds to the immersive experience, making every session feel meaningful and compelling.

Uncharted 4

Uncharted 4 is the best story-driven game I’ve ever finished. It was amazing, with incredible graphics and a gripping narrative that kept me hooked from start to finish. I completed it in one go because I couldn’t put it down.
